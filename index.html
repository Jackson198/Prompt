PromptMode

How many times have you been writing a paper and thought I wish there was something that could write this essay for me. Now there is with PromptMode what is PromptMode you ask? PromptMode is an artifical intellegence based software that writes your essays quickly and easily.

How Does it Work?

On creation of your account you will be prompted asking if you would like to write an essay each essay costs 1.99 or you can refer 10 people to use the service this will give you a free essay. Each person you refer will decrease the price of the essay by 20 cents. Once you have done one of the following you will be prompted your about your grade, number of pages, the subject of your essay, type of essay and some other questions pertaining to the type and subject of your essay.
